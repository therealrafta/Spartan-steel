Who We Are

Zimbabwe's Home Metalwork Specialists

Spartan Steel is a small, passionate team of skilled metalworkers based in Zimbabwe. We specialise in bespoke wrought iron and steel fabrication for residential clients — every piece we make is custom, crafted by hand to your exact requirements.

We believe your home deserves metalwork that's both beautiful and built to last. Whether it's a statement driveway gate, an ornate gazebo, a sturdy borehole tank stand, or elegant bedroom furniture — we bring the same care and precision to every job, big or small.

How It Works

From Idea to Installation

Four simple steps from your first enquiry to a finished product installed on your property.

01
Consultation & Quote
Tell us what you need. We visit your site or discuss via phone/WhatsApp, take measurements and provide a free no-obligation quote.
02
Design & Approval
We sketch your design and refine it until it's exactly what you envision. You approve before we build — no surprises.
03
Fabrication
Our team hand-crafts your piece in our workshop using quality iron and steel. We keep you updated throughout the process.
04
Delivery & Installation
We deliver and install on-site. We don't leave until you're completely satisfied with the finished result.
